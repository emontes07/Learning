<div class="section" id="sec-intro">
  <h2>📖 Introduction</h2>
  <p>Enhance your decision-making by using Microsoft 365 <strong>Copilot Chat</strong> to
gather insights, conduct analyses, and draft professional
communications. In the following exercises, you'll learn how to leverage
Copilot Chat across various Microsoft 365 apps to research information,
summarize content, analyze data, and generate documents.</p>
    <div class="callout"><strong>Note:</strong> Sample prompts are provided to help you get started. Feel<br>free to personalize them to suit your needs. If Copilot Chat doesn’t<br>deliver the result you want, refine your prompt and try again. Enjoy<br>the process and have fun experimenting!</div>
</div>

    <h2>Task 1: Perform Online Research & Analysis</h2>
    <span class="toggle-icon open">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body" id="body1">
    <p>Using <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>, gather insights on a relevant topic by analyzing
internal sources (such as emails, chat history, and SharePoint
documents). Then broaden your research with industry articles, external
reports, or relevant websites.</p>
    <p>If you don't have a specific internal topic to research, try focusing on
a publicly available topic of your choice – for example, <strong>technology
trends, market insights, customer experience</strong>, or <strong>compliance
requirements</strong>.</p>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Open a new browser tab and navigate to
<a href="https://m365copilot.com/" target="_blank">M365copilot.com</a>.</li>
      <li>Select <strong>Copilot Chat</strong> (the AI chat agent for Microsoft 365).</li>
      <li>Use a prompt to ask Copilot to research your chosen topic. For
example:</li>
    </ol>
    <p><strong>Sample Prompt</strong> <em>(if no internal topic is available)</em>:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">Provide a comprehensive regional update for [Your Region] to assist in planning our operations. 
The update should include the latest news and insights on the following aspects:

Demographic Trends:
- Population growth or decline
- Age distribution
- Income levels
- Consumer behavior patterns

Financial Events:
- Economic indicators (e.g., employment rates, inflation, GDP growth)
- Major business developments (e.g., new businesses opening, closures, expansions)
- Changes in consumer spending habits

Weather Events:
- Current weather conditions and forecasts
- Recent and upcoming severe weather events (e.g., storms, hurricanes, heatwaves)
- Impact of weather on local infrastructure and transportation

Please provide detailed information and relevant data sources for each aspect to support strategic decision-making for our operations.<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <div class="callout green"><strong>Tip:</strong> If the first response is too broad or not detailed enough,<br>refine your prompt by adding more context (for example, specify a<br>particular region, industry, or time frame).<br><br><strong>Learning Outcome:</strong>  <br>After completing this task, you'll be able to use Copilot Chat to<br>perform structured online research and gather actionable insights.</div>
  </div>
</div>

    <span class="toggle-icon">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body collapsed" id="body2">
    <p>Using <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>, transform raw meeting notes into a structured,
professional summary. This will help clearly communicate key decisions,
action items, and responsibilities—ensuring alignment across teams.</p>
    <p>If you don’t have your own meeting notes available, you can practice
with a sample transcript or text file that contains general meeting
content.</p>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Open a new browser tab and navigate to
<a href="https://m365copilot.com/" target="_blank">M365copilot.com</a>.</li>
      <li>Select <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>.</li>
      <li>Upload a text file containing your meeting notes (e.g. a transcript
of a meeting).</li>
    </ol>
    <p><strong>Sample Prompt</strong> <em>(after uploading your meeting notes)</em>:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">Using my meeting notes, create a formal meeting notes structure and align the content with a meeting title that encapsulates the meeting goal or agenda. 

The final output should format my notes with these sections:
- Title  
- Attendees  
- Goal  
- Topics to Discuss  
- Product Details  
- Tasks to be Completed  
- Timelines and Responsibilities  
- Resources  
- Q&amp;A and Next Steps<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <div class="callout"><strong>Note:</strong> You can ask Copilot to adjust the level of detail in the<br>summary. For example, try adding “make the summary more concise” or<br>“expand on the tasks to be completed” if needed.<br><br><strong>Learning Outcome:</strong>  <br>After completing this task, you'll know how to turn unstructured notes<br>into a professional, shareable meeting summary.</div>
  </div>
</div>

    <h2>Task 3: Refine Results with Follow-Up Questions</h2>
    <span class="toggle-icon">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body collapsed" id="body3">
    <p>Using <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>, practice enhancing your results by asking
follow-up questions. This task shows how you can go beyond the first
answer to structure data and obtain deeper, actionable insights for
decision-making.</p>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Open a new browser tab and navigate to
<a href="https://m365copilot.com/" target="_blank">M365copilot.com</a>.</li>
      <li>Select <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>.</li>
      <li>Enter the following series of prompts one by one to progressively
refine your research results:</li>
    </ol>
    <p><strong>Sample Prompt Sequence</strong>:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">Please identify trends in customer preferences in our market and use these insights to develop ideas for new products for our company.
Please structure those ideas in a table format and for each idea add a column for the potential target groups and ideas for an advertising campaign.
How can I prioritize these ideas effectively?<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <div class="callout green"><strong>Tip:</strong> Follow-up questions help refine and deepen your results. Try<br>clarifying prompts like: “Expand the table with additional product<br>ideas based on recent market trends,” “Which ideas might have the<br>highest ROI in the next 12 months?” or “Create a prioritization matrix<br>ranking these ideas by market demand, profitability, and alignment<br>with our brand values.”<br><br><strong>Learning Outcome:</strong>  <br>After completing this task, you’ll understand how to use follow-up<br>prompts to get richer insights and more actionable recommendations<br>from Copilot Chat.</div>
  </div>
</div>

    <h2>Task 4: Summarize and Recap Emails in Outlook</h2>
    <span class="toggle-icon">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body collapsed" id="body4">
    <p>Use <strong>Copilot Chat in Outlook</strong> to quickly understand the content of an
email and extract key actions. This task will help you summarize a long
email thread and clearly identify what is being requested — both from
you and from your team.</p>
    <p>This use case is perfect for staying on top of priorities, deadlines,
and next steps without manually reading through a lengthy email chain.</p>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Open <a href="https://outlook.office.com/" target="_blank">Outlook on the web</a> in your
browser.</li>
      <li>Select an email thread that contains multiple details, requests, or
deadlines.</li>
      <li>Click the <strong>Copilot</strong> icon in the upper-right corner of the Outlook
window.</li>
      <li>In the Copilot Chat panel that appears, enter the following prompt:</li>
    </ol>
    <p><strong>Sample Prompt</strong>:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">Recap my email, show me:
1. What is being asked
2. What is being asked of the team
3. What is being asked directly of me

If there are any dates or topics mentioned, put those in a table.<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <div class="callout green"><strong>💡 Tip:</strong> If your email thread is very long, try adding something<br>like “summarize in 3 bullet points” or “summarize in less than 100<br>words” to your prompt for a quicker, more focused summary.<br><br><strong>🎯 Learning Outcome:</strong>  <br>You will learn how to use Copilot Chat in Outlook to extract key<br>requests, action items, and deadlines from a single email or an entire<br>thread, helping you and your team stay organized and responsive.</div>
  </div>
</div>

    <span class="toggle-icon">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body collapsed" id="body5">
    <p>Use <strong>Copilot Chat</strong> to transform raw survey or review data into
actionable insights with charts and visualizations. In this task, you'll
use a <code>.csv</code> file (exported from Excel) and ask Copilot to identify
sentiment, uncover key themes, and generate visuals like a pie chart and
a word cloud.</p>
    <p>This task is perfect for analyzing customer feedback or product reviews
and creating compelling visuals for presentations or reports.</p>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Download the sample dataset <strong>Charger Reviews.csv</strong> (right-click the
link and choose "Save link as"): <a href="https://github.com/emontes07/Learning/blob/main/ResourceFiles/Charger%20Reviews.csv" target="_blank">Charger
Reviews.csv</a></li>
      <li>Open a new browser tab and go to
<a href="https://m365copilot.com/" target="_blank">M365copilot.com</a>, then select <strong>Copilot
Chat</strong>.</li>
      <li>Upload your downloaded <strong>Charger Reviews.csv</strong> file to Copilot Chat.</li>
      <li>Use the following sample prompt to analyze and visualize the data:</li>
    </ol>
    <p>screenshot showing sentiment analysis pie chart and word cloud</p>
    <p><strong>Sample Prompt</strong> <em>(after uploading your CSV file)</em>:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">For each review, analyze the whole sentence and provide a sentiment analysis.  
List the top 10 sentiments with counts based on the reviews using the attached file. Analyze the column “Reviews” located in column C.

Then, list each sentiment with its count of reviews and identify the top 10 themes mentioned in the reviews.  
Create a pie graph with Easter-themed colors based on the sentiment counts, and then create a word cloud based on the common themes and mentions.<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <div class="callout green"><strong>Tip:</strong> If you want to customize the visuals further, you can follow<br>up with questions. For example: *“Can you provide a downloadable<br>version of the word cloud?”<em> or </em>“Can you generate a more vibrant<br>color scheme for the pie chart?”<em> You could also ask, </em>“What software<br>can I use to recreate these visuals manually?”* for additional<br>guidance.<br><br><strong>Learning Outcome:</strong>  <br>After completing this task, you’ll be able to analyze qualitative data<br>from customer reviews or survey feedback, extract key themes and<br>sentiment, and create visual summaries using Copilot Chat.</div>
  </div>
</div>

    <h2>Task 9: (FYI): Create or Replace an Excel Macro with Copilot</h2>
    <span class="toggle-icon">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body collapsed" id="body9">
    <p>This advanced, <strong>FYI-only</strong> task demonstrates two ways to handle Excel
macro-style calculations using <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>: either by asking
Copilot to perform the calculation directly from an uploaded
spreadsheet, or by having Copilot help author or adjust a VBA macro for
Excel.</p>
    <div class="callout warn"><strong>Heads-up:</strong> VBA macros don’t run in <strong>Excel for the web</strong>, but<br>Copilot Chat can still perform the same calculations from your<br>uploaded workbook.</div>
    <p><strong>How to use it:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Open a new browser tab and go to
<a href="https://m365copilot.com/" target="_blank">M365copilot.com</a>.</li>
      <li>Select <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>.</li>
      <li>Proceed with one of the two options below.</li>
    </ol>
    <p>screenshot showing two macro approaches with Copilot</p>
    <p><strong>Option 1 – Have Copilot perform the calculation from your file</strong></p>
    <p>Upload your <code>Revenue.xlsx</code> (or a similar workbook with data) and ask
Copilot to perform the calculation directly, for example:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">Please create an Excel Macro script that performs the following task:  
Calculate the total and average revenue from the "Sales by Product" worksheet. 

For Excel web users, execute the equivalent calculation now on the uploaded file and report:  
- Total Revenue  
- Average Revenue<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <p><strong>Option 2 – Ask Copilot to draft/modify the VBA macro</strong></p>
    <p>Alternatively, you can have Copilot assist with the VBA code itself. For
example, ask Copilot:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">Write a VBA macro that calculates the total and average revenue from the "Sales by Product" worksheet and displays the results in a message box.

Now modify the script so it writes:  
- Total Revenue to cell J4  
- Average Revenue to cell J5  
and formats both as currency.<button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyPrompt(this)">Copy</button></div>
    <div class="callout green"><strong>Tip:</strong> Here are some follow-up requests you can try with Copilot in<br>this scenario:  <br>- Explain what this macro does line by line.  <br>- Convert this macro to a Copilot prompt that performs the same<br>calculation without VBA.  <br>- Add error handling if the specified sheet or column is missing.<br><br><strong>Learning Outcome:</strong>  <br>You’ll understand two practical approaches to handle macro-style tasks<br>with Copilot: (1) letting Copilot Chat run calculations directly on<br>your data, or (2) having Copilot author or refactor a VBA macro for<br>desktop Excel (including writing results to specific cells).</div>
  </div>
</div>

    <h2>Task 10: Create a Job Description & Evaluate a Candidate</h2>
    <span class="toggle-icon">▾</span>
  </div>
  <div class="part-body collapsed" id="body10">
    <p>Use <strong>Copilot Chat</strong> to create a professional job description based on
web research, define key performance indicators (KPIs) for the role, and
then analyze a candidate’s resume for fit against that role. This task
is ideal for HR teams, hiring managers, or recruiters who want to
streamline job description creation and resume evaluation.</p>
    <p><strong>Steps:</strong></p>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Open a new browser tab and navigate to
<a href="https://m365copilot.com/" target="_blank">M365copilot.com</a>.</li>
      <li>Select <strong>Copilot Chat</strong>.</li>
      <li>Start by asking Copilot to research and build a job profile (job
description and role details) for the position you have in mind.</li>
      <li>Next, upload a candidate’s resume (in <code>.docx</code> format) and ask
Copilot to perform a gap analysis comparing the resume to the job
requirements.</li>
    </ol>
    <p>screenshot showing job description creation and resume evaluation</p>
    <p><strong>Sample Prompt</strong> <em>(Step 1 – Build the job description)</em>:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">In a table format, perform a web analysis for a "data analyst" role in finance that includes:
- Common industry job titles for this role  
- A basic definition of the role  
- Key attributes or skills required  
- Ideal average years of education or experience  
- Typical salary range  

Also, provide a list of 10 interview questions **with answers** suitable for this role.
